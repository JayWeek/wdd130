History

Founded in 2010, Aquathrill started as a small group of adventure enthusiasts who shared a passion for white-water rafting. What began as a weekend hobby soon turned into a full-fledged company dedicated to providing thrilling and safe rafting experiences. With its first base camp set along the roaring rapids of the River Crest, Aquathrill quickly gained popularity among thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike.

Over the years, Aquathrill expanded its operations, introducing guided tours, safety training, and customized rafting expeditions. Today, it is known for offering some of the most exhilarating water adventures, blending adrenaline-pumping rapids with breathtaking scenic views. With a commitment to eco-friendly practices and top-notch safety measures, Aquathrill continues to push the boundaries of adventure, making every ride an unforgettable experience.
